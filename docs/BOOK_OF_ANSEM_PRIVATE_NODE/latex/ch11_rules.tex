\section{Operator modes (holder-based)}

\texttt{CONTROLLER\_WALLET} is the reference. Flip \textbf{MODE} on \texttt{/run}
(or \texttt{OPERATOR\_MODE} / \texttt{POST /api/operator-mode}):

\begin{tabular}{ll}
\textbf{Mode} & \textbf{What the bot does next} \\
\hline
\texttt{cover} & Min $\geq$5 ANSEM (and $\geq\sim\$1$ dual) in all controller top-N \\
\texttt{mirror} & Reaccumulate toward controller weights; propose take-outs when overweight \\
\texttt{ape} & Enter fresh TOKEN--ANSEM pairs at the pair-min floor \\
\texttt{hold} & No seed / no trim proposals \\
\end{tabular}

Fees stay on Setup \texttt{/#config} --- not a seed mode.

\textbf{Pair min:} $\max(\texttt{PAIR\_MIN\_ANSEM}, \texttt{PAIR\_MIN\_USD}/(2\times\text{ANSEM price}))$
so new entries are never sub-dollar dust.

\section{Capital floors}

\begin{tabular}{lrl}
\textbf{Floor} & \textbf{Default} & \textbf{Meaning} \\
\hline
Gas \texttt{SOL\_RESERVE} & 0.02 & Never spend \\
Operating & 0.05 & Block ATA/buys; prefer \texttt{topup\_sol} \\
Target & 0.08 & After ANSEM$\to$SOL recovery \\
Rent / pair & $\sim$0.008 & Left by capped \texttt{buy\_ansem} \\
Pair min USD & 1 & Dual-sided floor for new LPs \\
\end{tabular}

\textbf{Predictable trap:} dump all SOL $\to$ ANSEM, leave $\sim$0.005 $\to$ cannot open LPs
(and may not even top up). Send $\sim$0.05--0.1 SOL if critically low.

\section{Seed journey}

\begin{enumerate}
  \item Set MODE (\texttt{cover} $\to$ \texttt{mirror} when coverage done)
  \item \texttt{topup\_sol} if below operating floor
  \item Capped \texttt{buy\_ansem} (leave rent)
  \item \textbf{Wallet census} --- deposit index dust you already hold
  \item \texttt{buy\_token} only when no dust for that mint
  \item \texttt{deposit} --- mins first in \textbf{cover}; deepen gaps in \textbf{mirror}
  \item Sort = \textbf{dip24}; universe = \textbf{tracked\_top10}
\end{enumerate}
